## About us

**Zerix Network** is an independent development project built by passionate, self-driven developers.

We are not a large studio, we are a small team of enthusiasts who design and experiment with custom systems, infrastructure, and gameplay mechanics while continuously improving our technical skills.

Artificial intelligence is part of our workflow: We use modern AI tools to enhance productivity, analyze systems, and design more efficient and secure architectures **without replacing critical thinking or human creativity.**

## What we build

- Develop custom modular systems  
- Build and maintain optimized infrastructure  
- Experiment with performance-driven architectures  
- Use AI-assisted workflows to improve security and efficiency  

## Current Focus

Our main activity is the development of custom Minecraft Bedrock servers and related systems.

We actively work on:

- PocketMine-MP plugins & cores
- Utilities and tools related to Minecraft environments  
- Scripts for VPS management, automation, and data handling  
- Small experimental applications and internal tools
